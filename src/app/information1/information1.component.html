SOLAR ON GRID INVERTER

Also termed as Grid Tied and Grid Feed Solar Power System
Most common and widely used system in residential,commercial, utility scale projects.
The sytem is always connected to the public eletricity grid
During blockout in grids, the system will not generate electricity in view of terminating connection between grid and the system
Since no battery system or energy system coupled, it cannot provide backup during blockout conditions

SOLAR OFF GRID INVERTER

As the name indicates this type of systems are not connected To electricity grid.
This type of system requires a battery storage system to store the solar power and supply to the utility consumption in case of grid blockout.
Selecting the right capacity of battery system enables proper utilization of solar power and therby supply to utility consumption accordingly
Off grid systems are highly recommended in remote zones situated far away from electricity grids.
Latest developments in battery and inverter systems enable a growing market for off grid systems.
